⇒ What is natural language processing ?
• Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a subfield of artificial intelligence (AI) that focuses on the interaction between computers and human languages.
• The primary goal of NLP is to enable computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language in a way that is both meaningful and contextually relevant.
• NLP involves the application of computational techniques and algorithms to process, analyze, and derive insights from natural language data.

⇒ Brief overview of Natural Language Processing:
• Connects spoken and written language with machine understanding.
• Powered by chatbots, language translation apps, and virtual assistants.
• Enables computers to comprehend human language.
• Crucial in information age.

⇒ Importance of text analysis in NLP :
• Teaching machines to read, comprehend, and derive meaning from vast text data.
• Powers sentiment analysis of customer reviews and automatic categorization of news articles.

Let us talk about Bag of words now :



⇒ What is Bag of words?
The Bag of Words (BoW) model is a common and simple technique used in natural language processing (NLP) for representing textual data.
It is a way to convert text data into numerical vectors, making it suitable for machine learning algorithms.


⇒ Steps:
• Create a Vocabulary:Collect all unique words from the entire dataset to form the vocabulary.
• Vectorization:For each document in the dataset, create a vector by counting the occurrences of each word in the vocabulary within that document.
• Sparse Matrix: The resulting vectors are often sparse, as most documents use only a small subset of the entire vocabulary.


⇒ Limitations:
• Loss of Sequence Information:BoW discards the order and structure of words, which can be crucial in some contexts.
• High Dimensionality:The resulting vectors can be high-dimensional, especially for large vocabularies, leading to computational challenges.
• Ignoring Word Semantics:BoW treats each word independently and doesn't capture the semantic relationships between words.


⇒ BoW techniques :
• Converts text into manageable pieces for machine processing.
• Breaks down sentences and paragraphs into individual words.
• Counts word frequency to create a "bag" of words.
• Ignores grammar and word order, capturing text essence.
• Forms foundation for NLP tasks like sentiment analysis and topic modelling.
• Great starting point for NLP beginners.
